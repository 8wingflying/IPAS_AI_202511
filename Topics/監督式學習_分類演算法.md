# 多元分類(multiclass)

  - sklearn.multiclass 模組
    - https://scikit-learn.org/stable/modules/multiclass.html#multiclass-classification
    - https://scikit-learn.org/stable/api/sklearn.multiclass.html
    - 支援三種演算法:
